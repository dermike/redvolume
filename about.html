About Redvolume

The label was set up late 2006, by Mikael Jergefelt, with the simple goal of releasing all kinds of dubstep sounds. Based in Stockholm, there's also a special focus on Scandinavian talents. Check out the releases section to discover quality nordic beats from L-Wiz (Sweden), JSL aka RUF (Denmark) and Tes La Rok (Finland).
