    About the label

    The label was set up late 2006, by Mikael Jergefelt, with the simple goal of releasing all kinds of dubstep sounds. Based in Stockholm, there's also a special focus on Scandinavian talents. Check out the discography section to discover quality nordic beats from L-Wiz (Sweden), JSL aka RUF (Denmark) and Tes La Rok (Finland).

    Send an email to mike@redvolume.com to get in touch!

    License music

    We're always open to license music for other projects than our own, just get in touch. We'd love to hear about it! Check the discography section to see what we've done so far!

    For sync purposes, licenses can be purchased directly from SyncSquirrel.

    Send an email to mike@redvolume.com to get in touch!
