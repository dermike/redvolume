Winter of Dub

Added: 2014-02-03

2014 start off dubby with the debut release of Dub Archipelago. It's exclusive on Spotify and Juno this week, and out wide on February 10. Not to be missed if you're into either deep soulful dubstep or full on minimal dancefloor stormers.

Following in March, EQuBE blesses us with the label's first ever track with Swedish vocals (by Mr Noon). If that's too exotic for you, there's also an excellent instrumental dub mix. Check it out on Soundcloud until released on March 3.

Glitch out!

Added: 2013-06-12

This week started with the release of Stolen Mech's "Sundog for Basstard", whose style is a first here on Redvolume. Glitch with a touch of IDM. One second you're embraced by chilled out breaks and in the next, glitchy wobbles from all sides together with oozing synths. Stream it from Spotify, or buy from any of the stores to the left, e.g. Beatport.

Redvolume CD out now!

Added: 2008-07-28

Redvolume presents Catalogue Part One CD compilation! Collected on the disc, is the label's full output so far, together with some previously unreleased tunes from up and coming producers. This compilation might just be the most diverse collection of dubstep to date, capturing everything from industrial influences to ragga and vocals.

Opening track, "Mass Dampers" by Ekstrak, is upcoming on DJ/Rupture's mix-CD "Uproot". Nordic bass heroes L-Wiz and Tes La Rok show that it's not just a UK thing, side by side with Bristol producer, Forsaken, representing the UK scene outside of London. As for the vocals, Redvolume is proud to present Hit By A Rock's gorgeous remix of Mercury Prize nominee Susheela Raman's "Love Lies", hoping to take you on an inspiring journey into what we love and call dubstep.

View the tracklist and find out where to buy.
